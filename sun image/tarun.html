The tarun "Good Boy" – Story Summary "Good Boy" is a heartfelt story about the deep bond between a young boy named Max and his loyal dog, Buddy. From the moment Max finds the scruffy pup at a local shelter, the two are inseparable. Buddy is more than just a pet—he's Max's best friend, protector, and constant companion through the highs and lows of childhood. As the years pass, Max grows up and faces challenges like moving to a new town, .

making friends, and even dealing with loss. Through it all, Buddy is by his side, offering quiet support and unconditional love. When Buddy grows old and starts to slow down, Max realizes just how much his furry friend has shaped his life.
